> THE TRACK: Call It Rain is the kind of song you put on a bumper sticker and a manifesto at the same time. A swaggering, horn-stabbing protest ska anthem about the crowd that cheers louder the harder it gets fleeced — the believers who'd rather pour another sermon than admit the rain isn't rain.

Wiseman Records cut its teeth on HAMLIN LAKE FUNK and the heavy-bass faith. This one's a different weather pattern. It's the first single off The Brass Tax, the next full-length from the label, and it's a warning shot wrapped in a hook you'll be humming on the way home from getting hosed.

FOR FANS OF: The Specials, Rancid, Streetlight Manifesto, anyone who's ever watched a man sell them their own water back.
